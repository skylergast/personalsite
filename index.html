CHURCH

Antioch Ann Arbor

The sole purpose of our move to Michigan has been to plant a church that will reach Ann Arbor and Detroit. In August 2013, we moved here along with five other families to make this God-given dream become a reality. Antioch Ann Arbor is a Jesus loving church that truly believes that Jesus is the hope of the world, and that the local church can have a global impact. We are a part of the Antioch Ministries International church-planting organization.

My role on our team is the Media Director. I am in charge of all things that involve video, graphics, web, and sound. My weekly duties involve updating our sermon podcast, keeping the website up-to-date, building the Sunday service presentation, and running sound on Sunday morning.

Check out our church at antiocha2.org or visit us on a Sunday morning here in Ann Arbor!
